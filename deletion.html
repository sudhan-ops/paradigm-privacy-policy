Account Deletion Policy

Last updated: February 9, 2026

At Paradigm IFS, we value your privacy and provide a straightforward way for you to request the deletion of your account and associated data in compliance with Google Play's User Data policy.

How to Request Account Deletion

You can request account deletion through the following methods:

  • In-App: Log in to the Paradigm IFS app, navigate to Profile Settings > Account Security and select "Request Account Deletion".
  • Email: Send a request from your registered email address to privacy@paradigm-ifs.com with the subject "Paradigm IFS Account Deletion". Please include your Full Name and Employee ID.

Data Deletion and Retention

What is deleted?

Upon processing an account deletion request, the following information will be permanently removed from our active database:

  • Your login credentials (email and encrypted password)
  • Personal profile details (Name, Work Title, Photo)
  • Cached device identifiers
  • Application notification settings

What is retained?

Please note that for compliance with labor laws, financial regulations, and corporate auditing, certain records may be retained for a mandatory period (typically up to 7 years) after deletion:

  • Attendance Records: Historical check-in/check-out logs for payroll verification.
  • Location Logs: Historical location data attached to attendance verification.
  • Employment Records: Necessary documentation for legal and administrative purposes.

This retained data will be moved to a secure, encrypted archive and will no longer be used for active application functionality or tracking.

Processing Time

Account deletion requests are typically reviewed and processed within 7-14 business days. You will receive a confirmation email once the process is complete.
