Privacy Policy for Paradigm IFS

Last updated: February 9, 2026

1. Introduction

Paradigm IFS ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Paradigm IFS mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our employee management and attendance tracking application.

Important Notice: This app collects location data to enable attendance tracking and field staff verification even when the app is closed or not in use. This is essential for the core functionality of employee attendance monitoring.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Location Data

We collect precise location data (GPS coordinates) from your device:

  • When: Continuously during work hours, even when the app is in the background or closed
  • Why: To verify employee presence at assigned work locations and track attendance
  • How: Through GPS, Wi-Fi, and cellular network positioning

2.2 Camera and Media

We access your device camera to allow you to:

  • Upload work-related documents and photos
  • Capture attendance verification images

2.3 Personal Information

We collect and process:

  • Name and employee ID
  • Email address and phone number
  • Work schedule and attendance records
  • Task assignments and completion status

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data exclusively for:

  • Employee attendance tracking and verification
  • Field staff location monitoring during work hours
  • Work assignment management
  • Performance analytics and reporting
  • Compliance with labor regulations

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do NOT:

  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Share your location data for advertising purposes
  • Use your data for purposes unrelated to employment management

We MAY share data:

  • With your employer/organization administrators for legitimate business purposes
  • When required by law or legal process
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • Encrypted data transmission (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure cloud storage with access controls
  • Regular security audits and updates

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as:

  • You are an active employee
  • Required by labor laws and regulations (typically 3-7 years after employment ends)
  • Necessary for legal compliance or dispute resolution

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of data (subject to legal retention requirements)
  • Opt-out of location tracking (note: this may affect your employment status)

8. Background Location Tracking

Specific Disclosure: This app collects location data in the background to enable continuous attendance monitoring even when the app is not actively in use. This feature:

  • Runs during your scheduled work hours
  • Can be controlled through the app settings
  • Is essential for field staff attendance verification
  • Complies with your employment agreement

9. Children's Privacy

This app is intended for use by employees aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect data from individuals under 18.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of significant changes through the app or email.
